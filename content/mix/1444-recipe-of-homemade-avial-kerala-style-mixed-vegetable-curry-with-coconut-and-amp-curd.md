##### Instructions to make Avial Kerala style mixed vegetable curry with coconut &amp; curd:

1. Grind the grated coconut, cumin seeds, green chillies with sufficient water to a fine paste.
1. Cut the vegetables into 3 inch long strips &amp; washed them well.
1. In a kadai heat sufficient water &amp; add the vegetables. Cook the vegetables with turmeric powder &amp; salt.
1. When the vegetables are half cooked add the coconut,cuminseeds paste.
1. Then add the whipped yogurt to the curry &amp; cook for another few mints.
1. Garnish/ season with curry leaves.
