##### The ingredients needed to make Soft Molasses Cookies:

1. Get  Big mixing bowl
1. Prepare 1/2 cup Softened butter
1. Make ready 1/2 cup Vegetable shortening(not margarine)
1. Get 1 1/2 cup Sugar
1. Make ready 1/2 cup Molasses
1. Take 2 Eggs(lightly beaten)
1. Prepare  medium mixing bowl
1. Get 4 cup All-purpose flower
1. Get 1/2 tsp Salt
1. Make ready 2 1/4 tsp Baking soda
1. Make ready 2 1/4 tsp Ground ginger
1. Get 1 tsp Ground cloves
1. Make ready 1 1/2 tsp Ground cinnamon
1. Prepare 1 as needed Additional sugar




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Soft Molasses Cookies:

1. Set oven to preheat at 350°
1. In the large mixing bowl, cream together butter, shortening, and sugar until lightly colored and fluffy
1. beat in molasses and eggs; set aside.
1. in medium bowl combine flour(sifting nit needed), salt, baking soda, ginger, clothes, and cinnamon. Blend thoroughly with wire whisk
1. Gradually mix flour mixture into creamed ingredients until dough is blended and smooth.
1. Roll into 1 1/2 inch balls dip top in sugar; place 2 1/2 inches apart on greased cookie sheets.
1. bake for 11 minutes
