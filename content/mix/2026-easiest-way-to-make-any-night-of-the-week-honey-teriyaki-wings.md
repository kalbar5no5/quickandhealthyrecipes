##### The ingredients needed to make Honey Teriyaki Wings:

1. Prepare 2 1/2 lb chicken wings
1. Make ready 1 1/2 tsp adobo
1. Prepare 2 tsp garlic powder
1. Prepare 1 1/2 cup teriyaki sauce
1. Take 2 tbsp honey
1. Take 1 bunch chopped, chives
1. Get 2 dash olive oil




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Honey Teriyaki Wings:

1. Preheat oven to 400.
1. Season wings with adobo and garlic. Heat the oil in a medium pan and sear the wings until golden and crispy. Then place the wings on a baking pan.
1. Mix the teriyaki sauce with the honey and pour it over the wings making sure they get evenly coated.
1. Cook them for about 45 min. Enjoy!
