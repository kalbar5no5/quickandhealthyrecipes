##### The ingredients needed to make Aam ka Murabba:

1. Take 250 gm sugar
1. Make ready 1 tbspn ghee
1. Make ready  Cashew for garnish

##### Instructions to make Aam ka Murabba:

1. Wash the totapuri mangoes. Grate it using a medium sized grater. Make sure it&#39;s not too thin.
1. Take frying vessel or kadhai and put it on medium flame. Add little ghee to it. Add the grated mango. Mix it well.
1. Now you can add the sugar to it. Mix it well. Keep the flame on low flame so that it doesn&#39;t get burned. Keep mixing it well for 15-20 minutes.
1. Once its cooked well, (you can notice the Change in color of the mixture to slight golden or brown) turn off the flame. Keep it aside for cooling
1. Your aam ka Murabba is ready. Garnish with cashew. Store it in fridge and use it as and when you want.
