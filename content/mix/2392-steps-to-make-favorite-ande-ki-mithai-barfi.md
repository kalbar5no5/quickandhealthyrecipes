##### Instructions to make Ande ki Mithai/Barfi:

1. In a blender, add eggs, ghee, sugar and milk powder, blend them properly. You can whisk it with hand too.
1. In a wok, add egg mixture and cook it on low flame and keep stirring it until mixture become thick and crumbly. - Add food color, cardamom powder and mix well.
1. Cook until milk and sugar water dies up. - Add dry fruits and cook on high flame until oil comes on surface. - Make sure you don&#39;t burn the dish.
1. Transfer to a greased dish and flatten the top evenly with the back of the spoon. - Cut into desired shape. - Allow to cool. - Garnish with chopped dried fruits and serve.
